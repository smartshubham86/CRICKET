Player Name: Ishan Kishan

Description: Ishan Pranav Kumar Pandey Kishan, known as Ishan Kishan, (born 18 July 1998) is an Indian international cricketer who plays for Jharkhand in domestic cricket and Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League (IPL). He is a left-handed opening batsman and a wicket-keeper.[1] He was the captain of India's squad for the 2016 Under-19 Cricket World Cup.[2] He made his international debut for the India cricket team in March 2021 against England
